The Odin Project: Rock, Paper, Scissors

Welcome to the Rock Paper Scissors Game!

Here's how to play:

  1. You will compete against the computer.

  2. Please select one of the three option buttons: rock, paper, or scissors.

  3. The computer will make its choice randomly.

  4. The rules are simple:
    • rock beat scissors.
    • Scissors beat paper.
    • Paper beat rock.

  5. You will play 5 rounds against the computer. The player with the most wins will be the winner! Enjoy!
